The Saints Roleplay 2.0

Who are we?

The creators of The Saints Roleplay and The Saints: 1998 are proud to present The Saints Roleplay 2.0, a completely overhauled, fresh, and immersive roleplaying experience! Dive into our QB-Core framework server, designed to offer a lore-friendly environment inspired by the vibrant and dynamic Greater Los Angeles area. Whether you're a seasoned roleplayer or just starting, this is the city where your story begins.

How do you operate?

We’re live 24/7, so the city never sleeps! Whether you're a night owl or an early riser, there’s always action waiting for you.

Our server thrives on two core principles:

  • Social Connections: Meet, interact, and develop your character with other players who share your passion for roleplay.
  • Dynamic Economy: Make your mark in the city by earning your way through a balanced and thriving economy—start a business, hustle in the streets, or climb the corporate ladder. Your story, your way.

What does lore-friendly mean?

When we say “lore-friendly,” we mean it. The Saints Roleplay 2.0 operates within the familiar and immersive world of Grand Theft Auto. All assets, from vehicles to weapons, follow the branding and lore of the GTA universe, ensuring a seamless and immersive experience.
